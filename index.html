01 / about

Who I am

I'm a robotics engineer based in Abu Dhabi, with roots in Kerala, India. I work where mechanical hardware meets machine intelligence — currently building an AI-powered robotic refuelling system, one of the first certified deployments of its kind in the region.

My day-to-day spans training neural networks for perception, integrating depth and vision sensors, and orchestrating industrial robot arms through ROS 2. I care about systems that actually run in production, not just in simulation.

I learn by building. Whether it's writing a camera driver from scratch in C++ or migrating a legacy ROS 1 stack, I'd rather understand a system to its core than treat it as a black box.
